About the Event

Hack and Harvest 2024 aimed to revolutionize agricultural practices through technology. Over 72 intense hours, teams collaborated to create innovative solutions for sustainable farming, crop optimization, and food security.

Our mission was to bridge the gap between agriculture and cutting-edge technology, fostering solutions that could make a real difference in global food production and sustainability.

Event Goals:

Develop innovative solutions for pressing agricultural challenges

Foster collaboration between tech experts and agricultural specialists

Promote sustainable and efficient farming practices

Accelerate the adoption of technology in agriculture

Project Showcase

CropGuardian

DH
FA

Diego Herrera, Fatima Al-Sayegh

An integrated pest management system using AI-powered image recognition to identify crop diseases and suggest targeted treatments, reducing pesticide use by up to 60%.

SoilSage

OB
HY

Olivia Brown, Hiroshi Yamamoto

A machine learning algorithm that analyzes soil composition and local climate data to recommend optimal crop rotations and fertilizer applications, increasing yield while preserving soil health.

HarvestHero

ZM
AP

Zoe Martin, Andrei Popov

An autonomous harvesting robot capable of identifying ripe produce and gently harvesting it, reducing labor costs and food waste in the picking process.

AgroAlerts

KO
IJ

Kwame Osei, Ingrid Johansson

A predictive analytics platform that uses satellite imagery and IoT sensors to forecast weather patterns and potential natural disasters, allowing farmers to take preventive measures to protect their crops.
